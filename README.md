# üìä Inventory Sales Dashboard

### Turning Inventory Data into Business Decisions

## üìå Project Overview

Inventory mismanagement directly impacts profitability ‚Äî overstock ties down capital, while understock leads to lost sales and customer dissatisfaction.

This project presents a dynamic **Inventory Sales Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel** to help decision-makers monitor stock performance, identify risk areas, and improve procurement strategy.

The goal was not just to visualize numbers, but to translate raw inventory data into actionable business insights.

---

## üéØ Business Problem

The business needed clarity on:

* Which products are running below their reorder levels?
* Are we overstocking certain products?
* Which categories are most exposed to stock risk?
* Which suppliers contribute most to inventory volume?
* How is inventory distributed across warehouse locations?

Without structured analysis, these decisions rely on guesswork.

---

## üîç Key Business Insights

* **A significant portion of products are overstocked (60%)**, indicating excess capital tied up in inventory.
* The **Electrical and Hardware categories show the highest number of low-stock products**, suggesting supply imbalance.
* Several **high-value products require urgent restocking**, posing potential revenue risk.
* **Warehouse A holds the highest stock concentration**, highlighting uneven stock distribution.

These insights can guide:

* Smarter procurement planning
* Stock rebalancing across warehouses
* Supplier performance evaluation
* Working capital optimization

---

## üìà Dashboard Capabilities

* Interactive slicers for category filtering
* Real-time KPI tracking
* Identification of stock gaps
* High-value reorder monitoring
* Category-level risk assessment
* Supplier contribution analysis
* Overstock detection

The dashboard enables decision-makers to quickly move from data observation to strategic action.
